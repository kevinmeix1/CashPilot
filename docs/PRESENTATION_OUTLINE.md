# Presentation Outline

Target length: 3-5 minutes.

## Slide 1: Title

**CashPilot**
Xero-powered revenue and cash-flow action cockpit.

Talk track:

"Small businesses do not just need to track money. They need to know what to do today to grow it and avoid the next cash crunch."

## Slide 2: Problem

Most tools show what happened:

- overdue invoices
- historical reports
- account balances

Owners still have to decide:

- who to chase first
- what offer to send
- which action actually changes next month's cash position

## Slide 3: Solution

CashPilot uses Xero data to:

- forecast 30-90 day cash position
- detect future risk windows
- identify revenue opportunities
- draft customer/supplier actions
- queue everything for human approval

Key phrase:

"From accounting records to approved action plan."

## Slide 4: Xero API Usage

Show the Xero API footprint panel.

Highlight:

- OAuth 2.0 connection
- `xero-node` SDK
- contacts, invoices, payments, reports, items, bank transactions
- line items used to detect revenue opportunities

## Slide 5: Revenue Opportunities

Show the Revenue Opportunity Agent panel.

Examples:

- reactivate a dormant high-value customer
- convert repeat work into a retainer
- cross-sell an adjacent service
- recover likely late payment

## Slide 6: Before Vs After

Show the forecast chart.

Talk track:

"The app does not just say there is a problem. It shows whether the selected actions remove the risk."

## Slide 7: Forecast Intelligence

Show the Forecast Intelligence panel.

Highlight:

- daily time-series ledger
- customer payment-delay model
- Monte Carlo crunch simulation
- cash-driver attribution
- sensitivity tests such as "what if receipts slip by 7 days?"

Key phrase:

"This explains what is moving cash, not just where the line goes."

## Slide 8: Human Approval Queue

Show Pending Approval.

Every action includes:

- Xero evidence
- forecast/revenue impact
- draft message
- what happens on approval
- human-control note

## Slide 9: Why It Wins

CashPilot combines:

- Xero data analysis
- deterministic forecasting
- ML-style payment-delay and Monte Carlo risk modelling
- explainability for business cash drivers
- AI agent explanation and drafting
- proactive revenue action
- human-in-the-loop safety

Closing:

"This is not another dashboard to check. It is the daily operating queue for protecting and growing cash."
